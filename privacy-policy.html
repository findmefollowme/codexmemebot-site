Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-11

This application is used to research Polymarket and X data, monitor market-related signals, and optionally publish posts or send messages on behalf of the authenticated X account.

Data we access

  • Public X posts and user metadata available through the X API
  • Account-level actions explicitly authorized by the account owner
  • Local configuration and runtime logs required for automation

How data is used

  • To read X content relevant to Polymarket research
  • To publish or manage X posts authorized by the account owner
  • To analyze signals, trends, and interaction patterns
  • To maintain audit logs for automation status and debugging

Data storage

  • API credentials are stored locally by the operator and are not intended for public sharing
  • Operational logs and research outputs are stored in the local project workspace

Data sharing

  • We do not intentionally sell personal data
  • We do not intentionally share private messages or credentials with third parties, except as required by the X API or applicable law

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the operator of this application.
